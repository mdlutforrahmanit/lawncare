About Greenscape Lawn Care

Founded in 2013, Greenscape has been transforming lawns across Alabama with our professional lawn care services. We're a locally owned and operated business dedicated to helping homeowners achieve the lush, healthy lawn they've always wanted.

Our team of licensed lawn care specialists serves Birmingham, Hoover, Vestavia Hills, and surrounding communities with affordable, reliable service you can count on week after week.
